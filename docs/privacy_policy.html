Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Hemanth H I ("we", "us") handles information in the AI CCTV Android application ("App"). We take your privacy seriously. This app is designed to operate locally on your device — no personal data is sent to our servers.

1. Data We Collect and How

  • Camera & Video: The App accesses your device camera to stream and record video. Recordings are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload video footage to any external server.
  • Microphone / Audio: Audio is captured alongside video for surveillance purposes and stored locally on your device.
  • Biometric Data — Face Embeddings: If you use the face recognition feature, the App processes camera frames using on-device AI (ML Kit + MobileFaceNet) to generate facial embeddings (mathematical vectors). These embeddings are stored only on your device in the app's private storage. They are never transmitted, uploaded, or shared. You can delete all enrolled faces at any time from the Settings screen.
  • AI Analysis Logs: The LLM Monitor feature generates on-device text descriptions of camera scenes. These are stored in a local database with a 7-day automatic retention window and are never transmitted externally.
  • Network / Connection Data: The App uses WebRTC to establish a direct peer-to-peer connection between two of your own devices. Connection negotiation uses Firebase Realtime Database solely as a signaling channel (to exchange IP/session data). No video, audio, or personal data passes through Firebase.

2. Local-Only Storage

All sensitive data — video recordings, audio, face embeddings, and AI logs — resides in your device's private app storage. No data is backed up to Google Drive or any cloud service (Android backup is disabled for this app).

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Realtime Database (Google LLC): Used exclusively for WebRTC signaling (exchanging connection metadata between your two devices). No personal content is stored. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google ML Kit (on-device): Face detection runs entirely on-device. No data is sent to Google. See ML Kit Terms.
  • LiteRT / Gemma (on-device): LLM inference runs entirely on-device. No prompts or responses leave the device.

4. Permissions Explained

  • CAMERA: Core function — live surveillance and recording.
  • RECORD_AUDIO: Audio capture alongside video.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Alert you when motion or a known face is detected.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Restart the monitoring service after device reboot.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS: Required to keep the surveillance service running continuously in the background without being killed by Android's battery manager.
  • WAKE_LOCK: Keeps the CPU active during active monitoring sessions.
  • INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: LAN streaming, WebRTC, Firebase signaling, and on-device AI model download.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Recordings are stored until you delete them. AI Monitor logs are automatically deleted after 7 days. Face embeddings are deleted immediately when you remove a person from the Settings screen. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Security

All data is stored in the app's private directory (not accessible to other apps). Android backup is disabled. Network streams are limited to your local network.
